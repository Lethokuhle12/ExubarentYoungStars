About Us

Main Service

Grade 8-12, features from more than 5 local and private schools within our organisation, contribute tuition fees of R200 per month covering 4 subjects

Skills Development & Employment

We work with youth who excelled in specific subjects, many of whom are currently studying at universities like UNISA. They have flexible schedules and dedicate their time to offering extra classes to our learners on a volunteer basis. Our primary goal is to support students in achieving the necessary marks for admission into higher education institutions, ensuring they have a solid academic foundation for their future.

Mental Health Services

Foster a positive and inclusive school culture: Creating a supportive and inclusive environment where students feel valued, respected, and safe. Encourage open communication, empathy, and problem-solving skills among students and staff. Promote a culture of acceptance and understanding of mental health issues.

Career Expo

We help learners explore positive career paths by introducing them to various higher education institutions. For Grade 12 students, we also assist with applications to universities and colleges.

Donations

We often have food and school wear drives to help the learners that come from disadvantaged families, most commonly we used to offer sandwiches and fruits every Saturday for lunch but unfortunately we had to stop because of lack of financial support.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Vision

At Excruciating Young Stars Academy, our vision is to empower the youth through education, personal development, and community-building, inspired by the African spirit of Ubuntu. We aim to bridge academic and social gaps, reducing dropout rates by providing holistic support that nurtures both the mind and spirit of young learners. Our goal is to create an inclusive environment where students thrive academically and personally, unlocking their potential for higher education and future careers.

Our Mission

Founded in 2016, Excruciating Young Stars Academy is committed to providing academic excellence, fostering skills development, and addressing the mental and emotional well-being of learners in Grades 8-12. We focus on equipping students with the necessary resources, support, and guidance to succeed in their educational journey and beyond. By offering affordable tutoring, mentorship, and career guidance, we work to ensure that every student can access opportunities that lead to higher education and meaningful employment.

Despite the challenges we face, we are dedicated to expanding our services and making a difference in the lives of the youth, and we seek funding to continue our vital work. Your support will help us provide the necessary resources to continue uplifting and inspiring the next generation of leaders.
